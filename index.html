About

After 15+ years at various Wall Street banks, I handed in my spreadsheets for the chance to stay at home with my two children. In my last role at Barclays Capital in London, I'd made a jump from Finance to Technology and after a very short time in that new role, I felt like I'd been missing out on my true calling - just as I was leaving on what turned out to be a seven year career break.

Now I'm passionate about perfecting my tech skills, and I'm thrilled to see the "future of work" include more remote employment and the exploding gig economy. To be able to provide for my family (meaningful income!) and to be there for them (no communte!) is a benefit I do not overlook.

I'd be honored to be a part of helping you create something amazing.
